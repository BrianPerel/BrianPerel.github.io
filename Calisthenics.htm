I enjoy doing a bodyweight form of exercise called Calisthenics. Calisthenics is a hobby of mine that makes me fit, lean, healthy, and strong. It provides many benefits like discipline, coordination, cardiovascular-fitness, balance, agility, determination, incredible strength, and resistance. All that this activity requires is your body and gravity. Additional equipment include a pull up bar, rings, dip bars, and resistance bands.
