Calisthenics


I am interested in an activity called Calisthenics. It is a fun and revolutionary bodyweight strength-training activity. Here you will see my skills relating to this activity. Filled with innovative movements, I get to demonstrate tremendous athletic performance. Calisthenics is a hobby of mine that makes me fit, lean, healthy, and strong. It provides many benefits like discipline, coordination, cardiovascular-fitness, balance, agility, determination, incredible strength, and resistance. Every exercise offers dozens of variations bringing maximum toning to you. This includes handstands, levers, muscle ups, planches, one-arm pullup, L-sit, single-leg squats, and one-arm pushups. All that Calisthenics requires is your body and gravity. Additionally, a pull up bar, parallel bars, resistance bands, and gym rings will allow for a more advanced workout.
