Music at our Coffee Spot

Our Featured Performer


Come join us at the Coffee Spot shop and see music performer Dalton Mars. Professional Guitarist and singer, Dalton will perform pop and dance pieces. With over 8 years of experience, we bring the best in the business. Enjoy our signature coffee types and his wonderful performances.



The Music starts at 5:00 pm and ends at 9:00 pm on Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays
