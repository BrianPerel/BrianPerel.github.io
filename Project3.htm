Online Business Database (SQL)

For this project, I developed a database for an online business using Oracle's MySQL DBMS

- The business has employees employees identified by a unique employee number,
their first and last names, and a city where they are located

- The customers of the business are identified by a unique customer number,
their first and last names, and a city where they are located.

- The items being sold by the business are identified by a unique item number,
an item name, their price, and quantity in stock

- Each order placed by a customer is taken by an employee and is given a unique order number
