 Our Special Dragon Latte

    Recipe:

  1. Pour ½ cup of milk into a mug
  2. Heat the pitcher to around 150 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Brew the espresso, then place brewed espresso into mug
  4. Place ¹ teaspoon of brown sugar into mug
  5. Place ² teaspoons of cinnamon into mug along with ¹ teaspoon of flavored syrup

    Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Flavored syrup
  • Brewed espresso
