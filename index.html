Introduction


Hi, welcome to my portfolio website. My name is Brian, I am a student at Framingham State University studying Computer Science. I have a strong interest in computers and enjoy doing IT and programming. I currently work as an IT technician at the FSU ITS department. I enjoy traveling, photography, music, art, technology, martial arts, hiking, rock-climbing, biking, parkour, and calisthenics. Here you will find my completed work for the CSCI 108, Web Site Development course that I took in the Spring of 2019. This includes a lawn care website, a coffee shop website, my resume, a page about a company database project I built, and a page about a virtual machine I setup. The purpose of this portfolio is to display my web site development skills that I have developed throughout this course.


This was a course about HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It covers all the topics needed to create interactive web sites. I have also learned about the creative side of developing an attractive and likable website. This includes understanding what colors work together and what don't, choosing a good layout for a website, and the effects various shapes have on the asthetics of a webpage. I hope you enjoy my website!
